# Chaitanya Cold Process Soaps

Chaitanya is a handcrafted cold-process soap brand focused on gentle, natural skin care. The experience on this site is built around a simple promise: pure ingredients, thoughtful formulations, and soaps made by hand for everyday use.

This collection currently features three signature soaps, each designed for a different skin need and sensory profile.

| Soap | Skin Feel | Key Ingredients | Benefits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sandalwood | Warm and grounding | Pure sandalwood powder, cold-pressed coconut oil, olive oil, shea butter, sandalwood essential oil, raw honey | Deeply moisturizing, calming, and naturally nourishing with a warm glow |
| Aloe Vera | Cool and soothing | Fresh aloe vera gel, cold-pressed coconut oil, castor oil, vitamin E oil, tea tree essential oil, green tea extract | Hydrating, healing, and ideal for sensitive or sun-exposed skin |

## Why Customers Choose Chaitanya

- Handcrafted in small batches with care and attention
- Cold-processed to help retain natural glycerin
- Made without SLS, parabens, artificial fragrances, or preservatives
- Designed to be gentle on all skin types
- Cruelty-free and made with a clear focus on skin nourishment

## The Product Philosophy

Each bar is made with a specific skin benefit in mind. Rather than offering a generic soap line, Chaitanya emphasizes purposeful formulations:

- Sandalwood for softness, comfort, and a rich aromatic experience
- Aloe Vera for cooling hydration and everyday soothing
- Manjistha for brightness, clarity, and Ayurvedic-inspired care

The product story is centered on natural ingredients, traditional cold-process methods, and a handmade approach that feels personal rather than mass-produced.
